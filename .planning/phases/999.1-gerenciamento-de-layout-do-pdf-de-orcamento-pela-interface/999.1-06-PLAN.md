## STRIDE Threat Register

| Threat ID | Category | Component | Disposition | Mitigation Plan |
|-----------|----------|-----------|-------------|-----------------|
| T-999.1-18 | Information Disclosure | ADMIN_API_KEY vazando ao browser | mitigate | injetada so server-side via adminBackendFetch; proibido NEXT_PUBLIC_ (Pitfall 5) |
| T-999.1-19 | Spoofing | acesso direto a /api/admin/templates sem proxy | mitigate | backend exige x-admin-api-key (AdminAuthGuard, Plano 02); proxy e o unico portador da chave |
